Latest Project

iOS Health APP

The iOS Basic Health App is a user-friendly application that helps you monitor your health and fitness. It calculates your BMI and offers a variety of fitness exercises. Set goals, track progress, and stay motivated with personalized workouts.

Apparel Waste Management System

The Apparel Waste Management app is an image processing application that detects the shape of material leftovers and predicts innovative products based on their labels. By analyzing images of apparel waste, the app identifies the shape of the remnants using advanced computer vision techniques. It then extracts information from the labels to suggest creative product ideas aligned with the material properties. This app promotes sustainable fashion by reducing waste and fostering the development of new products from leftover materials.

Plant Monitoring system

Introducing our innovative IoT device for farmers, designed to minimize vulnerabilities in manual systems. This device helps maintain optimal environmental conditions for home gardens, particularly hydroponics crops. With sensors measuring soil and environment temperature, soil moisture, humidity, air quality, and light levels, the device transmits data to an ESP32 module. The module processes and uploads data to a server through an internet service provider, enabling farmers to access real-time information and analysis via a mobile or web application. Power is supplied via a 5V 1A connection to the device's 12V DC socket. Enhance plant health and productivity with our advanced IoT solution.
